Pancakes

Everyone loves pancakes. That is my favourite dish and I eat it every single day. It is easy to make and very delicious and good looking meal!

It`s very easy to prepare. All you need is a couple ingredients and frying pan. That`s not too hard, right?

Ingredients

  • plain flour
  • eggs
  • milk
  • sunflower
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug.
  2. Whisk to a smooth batter.
  3. Set aside for 30 mins to rest if you have time, or start cooking straight away.
  4. When you start cooking, do it until your pancakes become yellow.
  5. Put them on plate, add some honey or sugar and you are ready to eat!
